About Me

Hi! I'm Shinnosuke Yagi, a Senior at Stanford University pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and an M.S. in Statistics. My hometown is Kobe, Japan, where I spent most of my life up to high school. I'm interested in the intersection of statistics/data science with environmental science, finance, and sports.

Outside of academics, I'm a performing member of Stanford Taiko (Japanese Drumming Ensemble) and an academic chair for SJEC (Stanford Japan Exchange Conference). I also enjoy reading books, watching baseball, and playing turn-based strategy games.
