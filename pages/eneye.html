Project Summary

At eneye, a company developing a device to enhance energy audits, I worked on building an interactive dashboard using Dash and Plotly, allowing users to easily visualize the results of energy audits. My main contributions included designing a user-friendly interface for analyzing temperature distribution and other key metrics in buildings. I also optimized the preprocessing pipeline for 3D point cloud data by integrating AWS S3 and Lambda, automating data storage and processing. This streamlined workflow reduced manual intervention and improved the efficiency of generating insights from audit data. Through this work, I helped enhance eneye's ability to deliver actionable energy efficiency recommendations.
